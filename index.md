## Writing samples

### [Your Clover App Market Rating May Have Changed](https://medium.com/clover-platform-blog/clover-your-clover-app-market-rating-may-have-changed-heres-why-1772d3a0055b)

### [Don't Discount Surcharging](https://medium.com/clover-platform-blog/dont-discount-surcharging-914b66d54e16)

This blog announces Fiserv's addition of a credit surcharge program for Clover merchants and explains how this update impacts cash discount apps.
I offer guidance about how to avoid breaking credit card compliance by making a simple SDK call for each merchant.
